# ✨ NodelyPy SC Overlay — Features

> Your ultimate Star Citizen companion — works as a desktop overlay **and** a mobile app on your phone.

---

## ⏱ Timer & Stopwatch
Track quantum travel cooldowns, refinery jobs, or trade run times. Start, pause, lap, and reset with one tap. Includes quick-start countdown timers for 5, 10, 15, and 30 minutes.

## 💰 Live Trade Calculator
Stop guessing your profits. Pick a commodity from **live UEX prices**, enter your SCU, and instantly see your profit or loss. Includes a quick-reference list of the top cargo ships by SCU capacity.

## 💎 Live Mining Prices
All refined ore and hand-mineable gem prices pulled **live from UEX**. Top-value ores are highlighted so you always know what to mine. Ship-mineable and hand-mineable are separated for quick reference.

## 🚀 Full Ship Database
Browse every ship in the verse — searchable by name or manufacturer. See crew size, length, and SCU capacity at a glance. Tap any ship to open an embedded cargo grid viewer.

## 🔨 1,000+ Crafting Blueprints
The full crafting database pulled **live from game data**. Filter by type (Armor, Weapons, Attachments) and weight class (Light, Medium, Heavy). Every blueprint shows:
- 🕐 Craft time
- 📦 Full ingredient list with exact SCU quantities
- 📍 Where to obtain it — default unlock or which faction missions reward it

Search by item name, resource, or faction to find exactly what you need.

## 👤 Player Lookup
Look up any citizen by their handle. See their avatar, enlistment date, badge, and languages. Their main org is displayed in full — banner, logo, member count, archetype, commitment level, and focus areas.

## 🏛 Org Lookup
Search any organization by its Spectrum ID. View the full org profile — banner, logo, tags, stats, and focus areas. Browse the complete member list with ranks, roles, and star ratings. Tap any member to jump to their player profile.

## ⌨ Keybind Reference
Never forget a keybind again. Searchable list of essential Star Citizen controls organised by category — Flight, Combat, On Foot, and Mining.

## 📝 Quick Notes
A simple notepad for mission details, trade routes, coordinates, or targets. Auto-saves locally and persists between sessions.

---

## 🖥 Desktop Overlay (Electron)

- Transparent, always-on-top window that sits over your game
- **Ctrl+Shift+O** — Toggle visibility
- **Ctrl+Shift+P** — Toggle click-through mode
- Draggable titlebar — position it wherever you want

## 📱 Mobile App (PWA)

Install it straight from your phone's browser — no app store needed.

1. Open the link on your phone
2. **Android:** Menu → "Add to Home Screen"
3. **iPhone:** Share → "Add to Home Screen"

Works offline. Auto-updates when new versions are pushed.
